A plugin to evaluate mathematical expressions selected in visual mode and replace them 
with the result.

Examples:
5 + 4 - 9 -> 0.0
10 * 4 / 2 ** 3 -> 5.0
sin(1) * 10 -> 8.415
abs(-5) + 4 -> 9.0
sqrt(4) / 3 -> 0.6667
32,000 / 4 -> 8000.0
npv(0.04, [0, 100, 110, 120]) -> npv(0.04, [0, 100, 110, 120])  
irr([-1000, 30, 100, 400, 500, 500]) -> irr([-1000, 30, 100, 400, 500, 500])
